Welcome to Mamoru Yakuwa's website!!

Hi there ! I am Mamoru Yakuwa, an undergraduate student at the department of French literature and language, faculty of letters, Aoyama Gakuin University.
My reserch interests lie in phonetics/phonology and morphology or their interface. The central framework of my research are i) phonological theories, especially Optimality Theory (Prince and Smolensky 1993/2004) and other computational theories. ii) experimental phonoogy using online experiment method. The target languages in my research are mainly French and Japanese. I would like to reveal what knowledge about language sounds human beings have and how they are computing. The universality and identity of language sounds should be revealed by both independent and universal or comparative studies.
